THE CORRIB

“Good pints, good craic, and God help whoever's behind the bar.”

The shift never ends — you pour until the owner loses faith and sacks you.

The pour: hold the tap to the ¾ line, wait for the surge to settle (don't rush it!), then top off to just proud of the rim.
The rest: click punters to take orders, deliver pints and plates, wipe messes, and calm or cut off anyone getting rowdy — two rowdies side by side means a fight.
