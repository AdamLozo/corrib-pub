# The Corrib 🍺

A browser-based Irish pub bartending simulation game. You're the bartender at The Corrib — pour proper pints of Guinness, keep the regulars happy, and try not to get fired.

## Play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No build step, no dependencies — one file, straight from the tap.

## The Game

- **Pour the perfect pint** — a two-part Guinness pour: fill to three-quarters, let the surge settle (patience!), then top it off with a creamy head.
- **Keep the punters happy** — take drink and food orders, deliver fast, wipe down the bar, and watch those patience meters.
- **Mind the drink** — every pint raises a customer's drunkenness. Let two rowdy ones sit side by side and you'll have a fight on your hands.
- **Don't get fired** — the shift never ends. The owner is watching. Unhappy customers, fights, and a filthy bar all erode his confidence in you. When it hits zero, you're out the door.
